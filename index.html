How Mira Shape Works

Three steps from concept to a downloadable 3D asset — no manual retopology required.

01

Describe or Upload

Type a text prompt like "ergonomic chair base" or drag in a reference image. The AI interprets form, proportion, and style.

02

Generate & Refine

Mira Shape produces a watertight mesh in seconds. Adjust topology, symmetry, or detail level with iterative prompts.

03

Export & Use

Download in OBJ, FBX, GLB, or STL. The result is production-ready for game engines, CAD tools, or print pipelines.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about Mira Shape, its capabilities, and how the platform works.

What is Mira Shape?
Mira Shape is a generative AI platform that converts text descriptions and reference images into production-ready 3D meshes. It is built for designers, engineers, and creators who need fast geometry from natural input.
What file formats does Mira Shape support?
Mira Shape exports to OBJ, FBX, GLTF, GLB, STL, and STEP. All exports include UV coordinates, vertex colors, and material assignment slots.
Can I refine a generated shape?
Yes. You can iterate on any generated shape with follow-up prompts, region regeneration, symmetry locking, and polygon budget control without starting from scratch.
Do I need 3D modeling experience to use Mira Shape?
No. Mira Shape is designed for prompt-driven workflows. Describe the shape you want in natural language and the AI handles the geometry generation. Expert controls are available for fine-tuning.
What makes Mira Shape different from other AI 3D tools?
Mira Shape focuses on clean, production-ready topology — quad-dominant meshes, functional features (fillets, chamfers), and multi-format export. It is built for real pipelines, not just visualization.
